About

Fairy tales are often the first stories we're taught, the first passed down to us by our parents, grandparents and guardians.

They act as our shared origin, and the commonalities and differences between stories from distant countries paint an oral family tree between all peoples.

This site is dedicated not only to stories about lovelorn mermaids and glass slippers, but tales of fierce roks, firebirds and river spirits, crown jewels and hidden gems that deserve to shine through space and time.
